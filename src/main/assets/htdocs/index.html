Suited

Suited is a JavaScript library that enables you to turn documents into presentations. It allows you to present the document so that the presentation and full article is the same thing. Lecturers can use slides to teach the class and distribute the slide and the details at the same time. Your next business presentation can be published on the intranet, so that colleagues can read the detail in the form of an article and re-present it at the click of a button!

Suited gives you the ability to make slide presentations and have something useful to distribute. Traditional presentations have slides with more details embedded as speaker notes. Suited takes the opposite approach - a full document with embedded presentation slides. The slides can either be invisible in the final document or be parts of the final document

Embedded (Invisible) Slides

Use 'data-figure'

Slides can be embedded in the document so that it is only visible when in deck mode. This means you can summarize a particular paragraph by embedding a slide in the document. To do that you add a section right after the paragraph with the slide details. To hide it you add the data-slide. This puts the section into 'slide only' mode which hides it when viewing the full document.

The slide becomes visible when the document is placed in deck mode. There is two ways to do this.

  • press SHIFT+S (to make a captial S)
    • This cycles through all the modes
  • Change mode in URL to be mode=deck
